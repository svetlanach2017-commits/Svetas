# Model Critiques

**Detailed evaluation and critique of Grok AI responses** on financial, operational, ERP and business process topics.

### Purpose
This folder contains real examples of **Model Critiques** — structured human feedback used in RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) and AI alignment processes. 

Each critique includes:
- Original prompt
- Grok's response
- Detailed analysis of strengths and weaknesses
- Identification of hallucinations and logical gaps
- Specific recommendations for improvement
- Overall quality score

### Content
- **9 real critiques** covering key business areas:
  - Financial analysis & margin decline
  - Sales performance and forecasting
  - Logistics and supply chain optimization
  - Investment decision making
  - Cash flow management
  - Pricing strategy
  - Inventory management
  - Customer churn reduction
  - Budget variance analysis

### Value for AI Training
These critiques help improve Grok's reasoning, business understanding, factual accuracy and usefulness in professional contexts.

### File Naming Convention
`critique-[topic].md` — e.g., `critique-financial-margin.md`, `critique-sales-performance.md`
