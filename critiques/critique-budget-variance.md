# Model Critique: Budget Variance Analysis

**Prompt:**  
Marketing budget is overspent by 35%. What should we do?

**Grok Response:**  
Cut marketing expenses and reallocate budget.

**Critique:**

**Strengths:**  
- Acknowledged the overspend problem.

**Major Weaknesses:**  
- No analysis of ROI per marketing channel.  
- No distinction between good and bad overspend (e.g., high-performing campaigns).  
- Recommendation is too blunt and risks damaging revenue.  
- No forecasting of future impact or corrective actions with timelines.

**Recommended Improvement:**  
Perform detailed budget variance analysis by channel, calculate ROMI (Return on Marketing Investment), pause low-ROI activities and scale high-ROI ones. Develop corrective action plan with monthly review.

**Overall Score:** 3.6/10  
**Category:** Needs major improvement
