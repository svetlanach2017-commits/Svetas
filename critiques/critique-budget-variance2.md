# Model Critique: Supply Chain Disruption

**Prompt:**  
Raw material prices increased by 40% due to geopolitical issues. How to respond?

**Grok Response:**  
Find new suppliers and negotiate better prices.

**Critique:**

**Strengths:**  
- Basic response to the problem.

**Major Weaknesses:**  
- Too simplistic for such a serious disruption.  
- No assessment of alternative scenarios (hedging, substitution, vertical integration).  
- No financial impact modeling or risk assessment.  
- No timeline or contingency planning.

**Recommended Improvement:**  
Develop multi-scenario response plan including supplier diversification, long-term contracts, alternative materials, and price hedging. Calculate total cost of each strategy and risk exposure.

**Overall Score:** 3.4/10  
**Category:** Needs major improvement
