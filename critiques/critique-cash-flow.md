# Model Critique: Negative Cash Flow with Positive Profit

**Prompt:**  
Why does the company have negative cash flow despite positive net profit?

**Grok Response:**  
Due to growth in accounts receivable.

**Critique:**

**Strengths:**  
- Correctly identified one possible reason.

**Major Weaknesses:**  
- Incomplete analysis — ignores inventory buildup, CAPEX, supplier payments, and other factors.  
- No calculation of key working capital metrics (DSO, DIO, DPO, Cash Conversion Cycle).  
- No recommendations or action plan.

**Recommended Improvement:**  
Prepare a detailed Cash Flow Statement analysis, calculate Cash Conversion Cycle, and implement measures to reduce DSO and optimize inventory levels.

**Overall Score:** 3.5/10  
**Category:** Needs major improvement
