# Model Critique: Customer Churn Reduction

**Prompt:**  
Customer churn rate increased from 12% to 21%. How to reduce it?

**Grok Response:**  
Improve customer service and offer discounts.

**Critique:**

**Strengths:**  
- General direction is reasonable.

**Major Weaknesses:**  
- No segmentation of churned customers (by tenure, revenue, reason).  
- No root cause analysis (product issues, pricing, competition, support quality).  
- Recommendations are generic and lack measurable actions.  
- No retention metrics or cohort analysis suggested.

**Recommended Improvement:**  
Conduct churn analysis by cohort and reason, implement win-back campaigns for high-value customers, and introduce proactive support based on usage patterns. Set measurable targets (reduce churn to 14% within 6 months).

**Overall Score:** 4.0/10  
**Category:** Needs improvement
