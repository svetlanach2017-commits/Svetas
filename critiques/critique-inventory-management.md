# Model Critique: Inventory Management Problem

**Prompt:**  
We have high inventory levels but frequent stockouts. What should we do?

**Grok Response:**  
Reduce inventory and order more frequently.

**Critique:**

**Strengths:**  
- Identified the core conflict (high stock + stockouts).

**Major Weaknesses:**  
- Superficial recommendation without root cause analysis.  
- No calculation of current inventory turnover, safety stock, or EOQ.  
- Ignored demand variability and lead time issues.  
- No ABC/XYZ analysis or supplier reliability assessment.

**Recommended Improvement:**  
Perform ABC analysis, calculate optimal safety stock levels using demand forecasting, and implement replenishment system with reorder points. Target: improve inventory turnover while reducing stockouts below 5%.

**Overall Score:** 3.9/10  
**Category:** Needs significant improvement
