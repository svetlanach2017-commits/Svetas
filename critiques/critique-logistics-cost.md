# Model Critique: Logistics Cost Reduction

**Prompt:**  
How to reduce logistics costs by 20%?

**Grok Response:**  
Switch to cheaper carriers and optimize routes.

**Critique:**

**Strengths:**  
- Basic ideas are directionally correct.

**Major Weaknesses:**  
- Answer is overly simplistic and lacks analysis.  
- No breakdown of current cost structure (transport, warehousing, inventory holding).  
- No evaluation of risks (delivery reliability, service quality).  
- No calculation of potential savings or implementation timeline.

**Recommended Improvement:**  
Perform Total Logistics Cost analysis, calculate key metrics (cost per km, on-time delivery rate), implement TMS system, and renegotiate contracts with top carriers. Expected outcome: 18–25% cost reduction within 6 months.

**Overall Score:** 4.3/10  
**Category:** Needs improvement
