# Model Critique: Pricing Strategy Optimization

**Prompt:**  
Our gross margin is declining. Should we increase prices by 10%?

**Grok Response:**  
Yes, increasing prices is a good way to improve margin.

**Critique:**

**Strengths:**  
- Correctly identified the direct relationship between price and margin.

**Major Weaknesses:**  
- No price elasticity analysis or demand sensitivity assessment.  
- Ignored competitive landscape and potential loss of market share.  
- No segmentation by customer type or product category.  
- No financial modeling (impact on volume, revenue, profit).  
- No risk assessment or alternative strategies (cost reduction, product mix optimization).

**Recommended Improvement:**  
Conduct price elasticity study, competitor price benchmarking, and customer segmentation analysis. Build a financial model showing different scenarios (+5%, +10%, +15% price change) with projected revenue and margin impact.

**Overall Score:** 3.2/10  
**Category:** Needs major improvement
