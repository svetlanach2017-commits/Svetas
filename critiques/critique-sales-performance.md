# Model Critique: Sales Plan Execution Improvement

**Prompt:**  
How to improve sales plan fulfillment from 72% to 95%?

**Grok Response:**  
Improve manager motivation and conduct more training sessions.

**Critique:**

**Strengths:**  
- General direction (motivation and training) is reasonable.

**Major Weaknesses:**  
- No root cause analysis or data examination.  
- Ignores possible issues in forecasting, product portfolio, pricing, or sales funnel bottlenecks.  
- No specific action plan, timelines, or success metrics.  
- Recommendations are too generic and not actionable.

**Recommended Improvement:**  
Conduct ABC analysis of sales managers, map the sales funnel, identify main bottlenecks, implement weekly pipeline reviews, and introduce KPI-based bonus system. Target: +18–25% plan fulfillment within 3 months.

**Category:** Needs significant improvement
